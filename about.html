ABOUT BUNGAN-BUNGAN

The spring was first developed in 2017. At the beginning, the area was fenced off, but once people discovered how deep and refreshing the waters were, it quickly became a favorite spot. On the very first day, more than 500 people visited, and from then on, people came to swim every day.

The official opening took place in September 2017 , during the first week on a Saturday, and the spring has welcomed visitors ever since.

The land is owned by Venacio Delgado and Demitrio Atinza, while the resort operations are managed under LGU Tourism.

From its humble beginnings, the spring has grown into a popular destination, known for its natural charm and welcoming atmosphere that continues to attract both locals and visitors today.
